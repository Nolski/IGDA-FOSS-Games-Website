About

The goals of the FOSS and Free Content SIG are to:

  • Create and/or publish case studies
  • Propose presentations and workshops at industry events
  • Explore a branding/awareness campaign around games built with open technologies/practices/licenses
  • Create educational materials for the web and/or print
  • Create, promote, and support events like the Liberated Pixel Cup game challenge
